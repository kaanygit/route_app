Aziziye Mosque is an important architectural structure that reflects Konya's historical and cultural fabric. Built in 1868, the mosque exemplifies one of the elegant examples of Ottoman-period architecture. Its design embodies the characteristics of the classical Ottoman Mosque building tradition, while its interior decorations and calligraphic works reveal the aesthetic understanding of the period. Notably, the woodwork used in the mosque's mihrab and minbar reflects the intricacies of traditional Turkish arts, enriched with exquisite tiles and penwork. Considering that Aziziye Mosque plays a significant role not only as a place of worship but also in Konya's social and cultural life, this structure is of great importance for preserving the city's historical heritage and its transmission to future generations.
